Andrew Ryan Violinmaker

HANDMADE VIOLINS UTILIZING THE HISTORICAL ARCHIVE AND MODERN ACOUSTICAL RESEARCH

Trusted by soloists and orchestral musicians across the globe, Andrew Ryan's violins are celebrated for their exceptional ease of response, warmth, and a powerful, clear voice. A graduate of the Violin Making School of America and a member of the American Federation of Violin and Bow Makers, his expertise was honed over 17 years as workshop foreman at Boston's renowned Reuning and Son Violins. He now dedicates himself to creating new instruments, blending revered Cremonese traditions with modern acoustic research to achieve unparalleled results.
